      About Resident Evil Requiem

      Grace Ashcroft never expected her next case to take her back to where it all began. Sent to a crumbling hotel to probe a strange death, the same hotel where her mother died. She must face the past and untangle secrets buried since Raccoon City.

       
      • Play as Grace Ashcroft, an FBI analyst who must confront personal demons while uncovering the truth behind a new outbreak.
      • Switch between first and third-person views as you fight, investigate, and survive with limited supplies and sharp instincts.
      • Return to Raccoon City, the site of a dark outbreak and a secret government strike, where despair and danger still linger under the surface.
      • The ninth mainline entry offers a gripping story, cinematic tension, and deeply immersive gameplay shaped by years of evolution.
       

      In this quiet requiem, will truth bring peace, or more shadows?
